Brand voice

We are

+ Trustworthy — never vague about privacy
+ Respectful — Islamic values inform, not dictate
+ Direct — plain language, no jargon
+ Empowering — the user always decides
+ Globally inclusive — 12 languages day one

We are not

− Preachy or moralizing
− Exclusive — non-Muslims are welcome
− Corporate or cold
− Fearful (security as paranoia)
− Heavy or slow

Tagline options

1 Privacy first. Faith forward.
2 Your OS. Your values. Your data.
3 Open. Secure. Islamic-native.
4 Built for the Ummah. Open to the world.

Naming conventions

+ Halal OS — full name, first mention
+ halal-os — technical / CLI context
+ Amina — voice assistant (not "Siri")
− "HalalOS" — no camelCase
− "Halal" alone — too vague
